About us

It is known fact that children do their most important learning before the age five. Up to this age, for kids each morning is the dawn of another amazing adventure. Considering this fact, our focus is not only on developing academic skills, but also intellectual, emotional, linguistic, physical, social and moral skills that will ensure all-round development of children.

About Us

We believe that children are active learners, who learn best from interacting with nature, other children and adults in child-centered activities. We provides caring and trusting environment in which children can flourish as individual.

We have highly qualified, trained and experienced teachers, who are well equipped to mentor students in all aspect of learning and development. Our staff is meticulously selected and is extremely motivated to help students go beyond the obvious and facilitate a holistic development for all learners. We are driven by the true passion of turning toddlers into achievers!.

Guidelines

Admission Guidelines

1. Parents must provide a consent to the school authority to post their child's photographs/videos taken in school or outside during any school activity/event/trip on the Global Kids official pages whether on social media or our own website where it will be visible to public.

2. You must allow the child to visit & participate in events or trips planned/organized by the school authorities.

3. Parents must abide to the policies framed by the schools.

4. Please inform the school authority about any pre-existing physical, emotional issues or diseases that a child may have or is prone to.

5. The parent may also take a round of the school and understand how the little ones are nurtured beautifully with care and love.

6. School uniform and Books will be available at the school.

Our services

PLAY GROUP

Children start to explore the world around them as soon as they are born. At our school, the play group curriculum enables children to continue their exploration.Early experiences in the first few years of life are critical for acquisition.

NURSERY

Quality education in the early years of preschool sets a firm foundation in the overall development of a child. So here the nursery curriculum provides a right array of activities to help children move towards achieving their early learning goals.

KINDERGARTEN

The transition from preschool to primary school is a crucial time of change for both children as well as their parents. We ensures smooth transition to formal schooling with meaningful and progressive learning experiences.
